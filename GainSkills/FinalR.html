The final round of an interview process is typically the last stage before a hiring decision is made. It is designed to assess whether you are the best fit for the role, team, and company culture. This round often involves senior stakeholders, such as hiring managers, department heads, or even C-level executives, depending on the organization and role. Below is a detailed breakdown of what the final round usually entails:
